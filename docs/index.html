Poor Man's Styleguide

A frontend styleguide for the pragmatic

What is this?

It's a quick-and-dirty frontend styleguide, designed to be copied and pasted into your CMS.

Why?

You're not Twitter Bootstrap. You don't have the time or money to make a custom fancy-pants dynamic CSS pattern-library. And that's OK. You have other fish to fry.
